Motivation: 传统秘密共享中经销商完全控制每个用户的份额，但实际通信中份额会受信道扰动。本文旨在研究在经典-量子广播信道下，经销商无法完全控制用户接收份额时的秘密共享性能。

Method: 采用单调访问结构，经销商通过经典-量子广播信道编码传输秘密，用户接收受信道扰动的份额。推导了可实现的单次秘密共享速率和任意单调访问结构的逆界。

Result: 获得了任意单调访问结构的可实现单次秘密共享速率和逆界，进一步推导了二阶和渐近可实现速率。在需要所有份额恢复秘密的特殊情况下，结果与经典信道上的现有秘密共享容量一致。

Conclusion: 该研究扩展了秘密共享理论到信道扰动场景，为经典-量子广播信道下的秘密共享提供了理论基础和性能界限，在需要所有份额恢复秘密时与经典结果一致。

Abstract: We consider a secret sharing setting with a monotone access structure involving a control node and $L$ users, connected via a classical-quantum broadcast channel whose input is controlled by the control node, referred to as the dealer. Unlike traditional secret sharing settings, where the dealer fully controls the shares given to each user, in our model, the dealer encodes the secret for transmission over the broadcast channel. This means that the shares received by users are perturbed by the channel and are not fully controlled by the dealer. Our main results are achievable one-shot secret sharing rates, as well as converse bounds for arbitrary monotone access structures. We further derive second-order and asymptotic achievable rates for arbitrary monotone access structures. In the special case where all shares are required to recover the secret, we show that our result coincides with the existing secret sharing capacity over classical channels.
